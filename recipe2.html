Prepare your Pizza in 15 minutes

A Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a flat round bread base with vegetables, cheese, meat, etc. on top, which is cooked in an oven

Ingredients Required

    Ingredients:
      For the Dough:
  1. 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  2. 1 1/2 cups warm water (about 110째F or 45째C)
  3. 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  4. 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  5. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  6. 1 teaspoon salt
      7. For the Toppings:
  7. 1 cup pizza sauce
  8. 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  9. Your choice of toppings (such as pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, olives, etc.)
    10.

Instructions How To Make

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in the warm water. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the activated yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms..
  3. Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour if necessary to prevent sticking.
  4. First Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1-2 hours or until doubled in size..
  5. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  6. Shape the Dough: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each portion of dough into your desired pizza shape (round, rectangular, etc.).
  7. Add Toppings: Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza peel or lightly floured baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your choice of toppings.
  8. Bake: Carefully transfer the assembled pizza to the preheated oven (either directly onto the pizza stone or onto a baking sheet). Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted..
  9. Slice and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing into pieces. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!
    10. <
  10. Feel free to customize your pizza with your favorite toppings and experiment with different sauces and cheeses for unique flavor combinations. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
